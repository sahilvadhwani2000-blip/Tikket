Block Layout
Lower A–H surrounds the inner field (5 bays each). Upper J–R is the large outer ring (9 bays each, no "I" block). Block N is at the north end — straight-on view. South Premium, Presidential Gallery, and Premium Suites at the south end.
🎯
Best Value Seats
Block D Bay 1–5 and Block E Bay 2–6 — most recommended lower tier by fans. Block N Bay 2 upper is gold standard for straight-on view. Blocks N & E are BookMyShow's top-recommended pairing for visibility vs cost.
🏆
Trophy Ceremony
All post-match presentations, trophy lifts, and celebrations face South Premium. Presidential Gallery sits directly above. South Premium East has the best angle for the trophy moment.
☀️
Sun & Shade
70% of seats are under the canopy. Evening match at 7 PM means sun is manageable. Upper tier is essentially fully covered. A night Final will be about atmosphere more than shade.
🎙️
Atmosphere
North and South stands have the best acoustics. Mid-tier is optimal — not front row, not top row. For a World Cup Final at 1.3 lakh capacity, when the crowd goes, it goes. Visitors describe it as "jaw-dropping" and "electric."
⚠️
Must Know
Only 2 main gates for 1.3 lakh fans — arrive 90 min early. No powerbanks, earphones, bags, pens, umbrellas, coins. Sealed water bottles OK. Use Motera Metro Station — 1–2 km walk. Gate 3 for VIP/Premium.
🍛
Food & Facilities
Free RO water at concourse points. Food expensive inside. Top floor upper tier seats have steep angle — visibility issues per fan reviews. Corporate boxes have screens. Mixed washroom reviews — arrive prepared.
🔭
View Tips
Sit as close to the front of your tier as possible — rear seats have steep downward angle. Long off/long on positions offer the TV broadcast angle. Corner seats have limited full-field visibility.
